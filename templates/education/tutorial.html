Chapter 1: Recognizing Phishing

Learn to identify the telltale signs of phishing attempts:

  • Suspicious sender addresses
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Generic greetings
  • Suspicious links and attachments
⚠️ Phishing Example:
"URGENT: Your account will be suspended in 24 hours! Click here to verify: http://fake-bank.com"

Chapter 2: Protection Strategies

Best practices to stay safe:

  • Verify sender identity independently
  • Check URLs before clicking
  • Use two-factor authentication
  • Keep software updated
✅ Safe Practice:
Always type URLs directly or use bookmarks instead of clicking email links.

Chapter 3: Common Attack Types

  • Spear Phishing: Targeted attacks
  • Whaling: Targeting executives
  • Smishing: SMS phishing
  • Vishing: Voice phishing

Chapter 4: What to Do

If you suspect phishing:

  1. Don't click any links
  2. Don't download attachments
  3. Report to IT security
  4. Delete the message
  5. Change passwords if compromised
