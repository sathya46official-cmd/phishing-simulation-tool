Cybersecurity Guidelines

Essential practices for staying safe online

Email Security Best Practices
  • ✅ Verify sender identity
    Contact the sender through a different channel to confirm legitimacy
  • ✅ Check URLs carefully
    Hover over links to see the actual destination before clicking
  • ✅ Use two-factor authentication
    Enable 2FA on all important accounts
  • ✅ Keep software updated
    Install security updates promptly
Red Flags to Watch For
  • ⚠️ Urgent language
    "Act now or your account will be closed!"
  • ⚠️ Generic greetings
    "Dear Customer" instead of your actual name
  • ⚠️ Suspicious attachments
    Unexpected files, especially .exe or .zip
  • ⚠️ Poor grammar/spelling
    Professional organizations rarely have typos
Incident Response Checklist
If you suspect phishing:
  1. Don't click any links or download attachments
  2. Don't provide any personal information
  3. Report the email to your IT security team
  4. Delete the suspicious email
  5. Run a security scan on your device
If you think you've been compromised:
  1. Change all your passwords immediately
  2. Check your accounts for unauthorized activity
  3. Contact your bank if financial info was involved
  4. Report the incident to authorities if necessary
  5. Monitor your credit reports
