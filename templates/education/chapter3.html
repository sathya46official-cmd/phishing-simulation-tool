Chapter 3: Common Attack Types

Types of Phishing Attacks

Spear Phishing

Target: Specific individuals or organizations

Method: Highly personalized attacks using researched information

Example: Email appearing to be from your CEO asking for urgent wire transfer
Smishing (SMS Phishing)

Target: Mobile phone users

Method: Fraudulent text messages

Example: "Your bank account has been compromised. Click here to secure it: [malicious link]"
Whaling

Target: High-profile executives

Method: Sophisticated attacks targeting C-level executives

Example: Fake legal subpoena or regulatory compliance request
Vishing (Voice Phishing)

Target: Phone users

Method: Fraudulent phone calls

Example: Caller claiming to be from your bank asking for account verification

Attack Progression

1. Reconnaissance

Attackers research targets

2. Delivery

Malicious email sent

3. Exploitation

User clicks/responds

4. Access

System compromised
