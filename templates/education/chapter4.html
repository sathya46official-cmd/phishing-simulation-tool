Chapter 4: What to Do If You Suspect Phishing

Immediate Response Steps

If You Receive a Suspicious Email:
  1. Don't click any links
    Avoid clicking on any links or buttons in the email
  2. Don't download attachments
    Attachments may contain malware
  3. Report to IT security
    Forward the email to your IT security team
  4. Delete the message
    Remove it from your inbox and trash
If You Think You've Been Compromised:
  1. Change passwords immediately
    Start with the most critical accounts
  2. Check account activity
    Look for unauthorized transactions or changes
  3. Contact your bank
    If financial information was involved
  4. Run security scans
    Use antivirus and anti-malware tools
  5. Monitor credit reports
    Watch for signs of identity theft

Reporting Channels

Internal IT Team

Report to your organization's IT security team

Anti-Phishing Groups

Forward to phishing@antiphishing.org

Government Agencies

Report to IC3.gov or local cybercrime units
